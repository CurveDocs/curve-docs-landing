  • Stableswap

    A highly efficient Automated Market Making Algorithm for multiple pegged assets utilizing the Stableswap invariant.

  • Cryptoswap

    Automated Market Making Algorithm for multiple volatile assets.

  • $crvUSD

    Curve Stablecoin using crypto-tokenized collaterals. Liquidations are supported by LLAMMA (Lending Liquidation AMM Algorithm).

  • Gauges

    Gauges serve as a mechanism to incentivize and reward liquidity provision based on liquidity provided and the community's governance decisions.

  • Pool Factory

    Permissionless deployment of Curve liquidity pools and gauges.

  • Fee Collection and Distribution

    Exchange contracts can charge an admin fee, which is converted to 3CRV and forwarded into a fee distributor contract, a process known as "burning". Fees are then distributed to veCRV holders.
