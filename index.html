CurveDocs

  • Stableswap

    DeFi leading AMM (Automated Market Maker), provides high liquidity and low fee transactions for pegged assets.

  • Cryptoswap

    Curve pools holding assets with different prices, allowing efficient trading and low risk for non-pegged assets.

  • $crvUSD

    Mint crvUSD using crypto-tokenized collaterals. Liquidations are supported by LLAMMA (Lending Liquidation AMM Algorithm).

  • Gauges

    Gauges measure the liquidity provided in Curve's pools by different users. More liquidity equals more rewards.

  • Fee Burning

    Exchange contracts can charge an admin fee, which is converted to 3CRV and forwarded into a fee distributor contract, a process known as "burning".

  • DAO

    Oversees the operations of exchange contracts, manages fee withdrawals, and ensures smooth distribution through the fee distributor contract.
